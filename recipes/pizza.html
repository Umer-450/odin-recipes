The Best Cheesy Pizza Recipe

I guarantee that this recipe is the simplest yet, best cheesy pizza you will ever create!

You'll only need 3 simple ingredients and a microwave or hot surface.

Ingredients

  • Naan Bread
  • Tomato Sauce (Or pasta sauce)
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Place the naan bread on a flat surface
  2. Take a spoonful of tomato sauce and spread it evenly on the naan bread in a circular motion
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of naan bread
  4. Place the pizza in the microwave and heat it up for 1 minute
  5. Take the pizza out and let it sit for 3 minutes before serving
  6. Enjoy!
